Installing Xampp (windows ver.)


  • Download 'Xampp' and open the executable.
  • Just press the 'NEXT' button. I will read the article just in case.

Installing Composer (windows ver.)


  • Download 'Composer' and open the executable.
  • Just press the 'NEXT' button. I will read the article just in case.

Open a Command Prompt

Execute a command


composer create-project laravel/laravel --prefer-dist Sample
  • The name doesn't have to be 'Sample'.

cd Sample
  • Go to the project you created.

php -S localhost:8000 -t public
  • Check the page by entering 'localhost:8000' in your browser.

Open VScode

  • Open the folder with VScode.

Modify routes/web.php

Finish

  • ctrl -C
